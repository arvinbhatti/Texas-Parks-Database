Chisos Basin Campground

Description

The Chisos Basin Campground is surrounded by tall, rocky cliffs and conveniently located near some of the park's most spectacular and popular trails

Operating Hours
  • Year-round
Elevation
  • 5,400 feet
Campground Map
  • Map of campsites

Details

60-site campground (no hook-ups). $14 per night ($7 per night with a Senior or Access pass). Flush toilets, running water, grills, picnic tables, and dump station. Trailers over 20' and RV's over 24' are not recommended due to narrow, winding road to the Basin and small campsites at this campground.
