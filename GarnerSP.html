Garner State Park

Description

Garner State Park is a state park in the community of Concan, Texas located in Uvalde County, Texas. Garner State Park, in the Texas Hill Country, is the most popular state park in Texas for overnight camping.

Operating Hours
  • Open daily from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m.
Size
  • 1,410 acres

Campgrounds

  • Full Hookup Campsites (New Garner)
  • Campsites with Electricity (Old Garner)
  • Campsites with Electricity (New Garner | Rio Frio)
  • Campsites with Electricity (New Garner)
  • Campsites with Water (Old Garner)
  • Campsites with Water (New Garner)
