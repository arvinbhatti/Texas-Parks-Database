Barton Creek Wilderness Park

Description

This expansive park located in south-central Austin along Barton Creek is popular with hikers and bikers for its 12.68 miles of trails. During the summer, Barton Creek becomes a popular place for swimming.

Operating Hours
  • All day, Year-round
Size
  • 1.26 square miles
Campground Map
  • Map of entrances

Details

Contains 12.68 miles of trails, with the main trail spanning about 7 miles. Mountain biking trails are plentiful and there are many limestone bluffs for rock climbing. In the summer, the creek open ups its swimming holes for locals to enjoy.
