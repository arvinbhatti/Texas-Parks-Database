Pease Park

Description

Austin's Pease Park is the West Campus neighboorhood in downtown Austin. Pease Park is known for its live music, family activities, and its annual Eeyore's Birthday Party from Winnie-tho-Pooh. The park sits on Shoal Creek which flows into the Colorodo River.

Operating Hours
  • Open daily from 5 a.m. to 10 p.m.
Size
  • 42 acres
Campground Map
  • Map of entrances

Details

Contains 12.68 miles of trails, with the main trail spanning about 7 miles. Mountain biking trails are plentiful and there are many limestone bluffs for rock climbing. In the summer, the creek open ups its swimming holes for locals to enjoy.
