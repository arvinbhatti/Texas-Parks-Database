Cottenwood Group Campground

Description

Cottonwood Campground is a quiet, shady desert oasis located between the Castolon Historic District and scenic Santa Elena Canyon.

Operating Hours
  • Year-round
Elevation
  • 2,169 ft.

Details

24-site campground (no hook-ups). $14 per night ($7 with Senior or Access pass). Pit toilets, picnic tables, grills, and water are available. No dump station, no generators allowed. A small picnic area is available across from campsite #23.
